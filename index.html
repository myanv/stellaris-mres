Engineering the cosmos

In pursuit of unparalleled scientific and engineering excellence, the Megastructural Research and Engineering Science Division, or MRES, dedicates itself to the relentless exploration and exploitation of the unknown. Our mission: to engineer the cosmos, encasing stars in colossal Dyson megastructures in a testament to Humanity's technological prowess and ambition.

Our Ethos

Breaching new frontiers

In the inaugural strides of 2220, Humanity tentatively ventured beyond our Mother system, Sol. Following Man's first voyage to Alpha Centauri, we stand on the precipice of a new era, in which we must thrust ourselves into uncharted frontiers; to boldly go, unhindered by fear of the unknown.
